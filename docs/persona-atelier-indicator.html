Blaize Bazaar · Personas · The Atelier indicator

Whose data, visible always.

Inside the Atelier, the persona indicator becomes a small persistent line in every architecture page's header — right of the breadcrumb. Tiny, mono-set, but load-bearing: in a workshop where the same page is shown for three different personas, it's the only signal that immediately disambiguates whose session you're inspecting.

The line itself

A small mono indicator, three states.

Right-aligned in the breadcrumb row. 22px italic-monogram avatar plus the persona name. Mono "as" prefix in ink-4 small caps. Italic Fraunces name in ink-1.

Marco · returning
· AS M Marco
Espresso-filled avatar with italic monogram. Used when a persona with curated history is active. Click opens the persona switcher modal — same one as the storefront.
Anna · gift-giver
· AS A Anna
Slightly different espresso shade per persona — just enough variation that a glance disambiguates Marco from Anna without reading the name. Avatar shade is the only point of variation; rest of the line is identical.
A new visitor · empty
· AS · a new visitor
Dashed transparent avatar with a faint center dot. Italic name in ink-4 (lighter than the espresso names). The "honest empty" state — visually distinct enough that participants see the absence of identity, not just read it.

Universal across pages

Same indicator, every page.

The line is rendered by the shared DetailPageShell component — every architecture page gets it for free. No per-page wiring, no inconsistency. If the persona changes, every page in the Atelier updates simultaneously.

Three rules · the indicator

Why this is tiny on purpose.

QUIET
Lives in the crumb row, not the header.
The page title is the page; the persona is metadata. Big persona indicators turn every architecture page into "the personas page," which it isn't.
PERSISTENT
Same place, every page.
Right end of the breadcrumb row. Always. Memory, Skills, Grounding — same line, same place, same render. Predictability beats decoration.
CLICKABLE
Doubles as the switcher.
Click anywhere on the indicator pill — same modal as the storefront. The Atelier doesn't get its own switcher; it shares the storefront's.

Tiny indicator, large work. · Rendered by the shared shell once · inherited by every architecture page. · Whose data, visible always.
