The full pattern

A summary of decisions encoded in this redesign — useful as the visual contract for the implementation prompt.

Removed
The inline search bar overlaying the cover photo. Redundant with the floating pill, visually competed with the photography.
Kept loud
Floating Ask Blaize pill bottom-right with ⌘K hint. The unambiguous entry point.
Kept inviting
Suggestion pills below the cover. Click → drawer slides in with that query already populated and streaming.
Drawer width
~480px on desktop. Right-side. Slides from the right at 200–250ms ease-out.
Backdrop
Espresso veil at 35% opacity. Storefront stays visible, dimmed. Chatting and browsing happen in the same experience, not two modes.
Persona indicator
Subtle · as Marco mark in drawer header meta row. Same convention as the Atelier breadcrumb.
Persistence
Conversation persists within session. Close doesn't clear; reopen resumes. Floating pill hides while drawer is open.
Dismissal
Esc, click outside on dimmed area, or X button. Three exits, all natural.
